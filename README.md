## Features

- **Extensive Pizza Menu:** Explore our diverse menu featuring a wide range of pizza options, from traditional classics to unique specialty pies.

- **Easy Cart Management:** Add your desired pizzas to the cart, adjust quantities, and view the total cost before placing your order.

- **Quick Ordering:** Streamline the ordering process by providing delivery details, including your name and address, making it effortless to enjoy your favorite pizzas.

- **Location-Based Address:** Utilize the "Use Location" button to automatically input your address based on your device's location, ensuring accuracy and convenience.

- **Order ID:** Each order generates a unique order ID for easy tracking and reference.

- **Priority Orders:** Opt for priority delivery to have your pizza order expedited for a small additional fee.

- **Currency Localization:** Experience pricing in your local currency, dynamically adjusted based on your location, providing transparency and ease of payment.

- **React Router for Navigation:** Our web app uses React Router for seamless navigation between pages, ensuring a smooth user experience.

- **State Management with Redux Toolkit:** Redux Toolkit is employed for efficient state management, enhancing the performance and reliability of our pizza ordering system.

## How to Use

1. **Order Your Pizza:** Begin your pizza journey by visiting the Fast React Pizza homepage. Browse the menu, select your favorite pizzas, and add them to your cart.

2. **Customize Your Order:** Customize your order by adjusting the quantity of each pizza in your cart. Review your order to ensure it's just the way you want it.

3. **Provide Delivery Details:** Proceed to the checkout, where you can provide your delivery details, including your name and address. Use the "Use Location" button for quick and accurate address input.

4. **Priority Option:** If you're in a hurry, choose the priority option to have your pizza delivered faster for a small additional fee.

5. **Currency Conversion:** Our system automatically converts prices to your local currency, so you know exactly what you're paying.
